Stir-Fried Spaghetti Recipe

Dish Name: Stir-Fried Spaghetti

Brief Description: A delicious and quick spaghetti dish stir-fried with vegetables, peppers, and seasoning. It’s a colorful, tasty meal that can be made in under 30 minutes.

Recipe Information

Preparation time: 15 minutes
Cooking time: 20 minutes
Number of servings: 3
Difficulty level: Beginner

Ingredients

  • 250g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion (sliced)
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 green bell pepper (sliced)
  • 2 medium carrots (sliced into strips)
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt (to taste)
  • ½ teaspoon curry powder
  • ½ teaspoon thyme

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until it’s just cooked (al dente). Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pan, then sauté the sliced onions until fragrant.
  3. Add the bell peppers and carrots, stir-fry for 3–4 minutes to keep them slightly crunchy.
  4. Crumble in the seasoning cubes, add curry powder, thyme, and a little salt. Stir well to combine.
  5. Add the boiled spaghetti into the pan and toss quickly to avoid sticking until the pasta is fully coated with the vegetables and seasoning.

Nutrition Facts (per serving)

  • Calories: 320
  • Protein: 9g
  • Carbohydrates: 50g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Fiber: 4g

Tips / Notes

Do not overcook the spaghetti; it should be slightly firm before stir-frying. You can add proteins like shredded chicken, beef, or sausages for a richer meal. For extra flavor, use a splash of soy sauce or oyster sauce.
